Quality Assurance Standards (QAS) / Auditor Training

 

The Quality Assurance Standards (QAS) for Forensic DNA Testing Laboratories and the Quality Assurance Standards for DNA Databasing Laboratories were issued on July 1, 2009 by the FBI Director. The standards describe the quality assurance requirements that a laboratory performing DNA analysis or utilizing the Combined DNA Index System (CODIS) shall follow to ensure the quality and integrity of the data generated by the laboratory. These standards also apply to vendor laboratories that provide DNA analysis services to another laboratory or agency but do not take ownership of the DNA data for purposes of entry into CODIS.

The QAS/Auditor training sponsored by the FBI is a computer-based training (CBT) course that familiarizes the participants with the forensic and databasing standards, as well as techniques for preparing and performing QAS audits, and identifies documentation requirements for demonstrating compliance with the standards. Current or previously qualified DNA analysts are required to successfully complete this course in order to be qualified to conduct QAS audits.   

This training page provides a standalone version of the QAS / Auditor Training. It is intended for non-governmental, commercial and private forensic and DNA databasing laboratories that do not have access to this training on the CODIS network.

This training requires INTERNET EXPLORER version 10 or above

It is not compatible with other web browsers such as CHROME, FIREFOX, SAFARI, or OPERA

TRAINING MODULES

Module 1 - Quality Assurance Standards & Auditing  

Module 2 - Quality Assurance Standards 3 & 4

Module 3 - Quality Assurance Standard 5

Module 4 - Quality Assurance Standard 6, 7, & 8

Module 5 - Quality Assurance Standard 9, 10, & 11  

Module 6 - Quality Assurance Standard 12, 13, & 14  

Module 7 - Forensic Laboratory Standards 15, 16, & 17   

Module 8 - Databasing Laboratory Standards & Audit

End-of-Course Exam  

EXAM SCORE OF 85% OR ABOVE IS REQUIRED TO COMPLETE THIS TRAINING  

IMPORTANT 

  • Your progress with each standalone training module cannot be saved. You are responsible for tracking your overall progress and working through the training modules at your own pace.

  • These training modules do not contain active hyperlinks. 

  • The most recent version of the Quality Assurance Standards documents are available at:   

https://www.fbi.gov/services/laboratory/biometric-analysis/codis

  • In order to complete this training and obtain a Certificate of Completion, you must complete all of the training modules and pass the end-of-course exam with a score of 85% or above.

 

If you have any questions or need any assistance, please contact QAS@FBI.GOV
